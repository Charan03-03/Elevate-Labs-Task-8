## Project Overview

This repository contains the Power BI files and source data for a comprehensive analysis of the Superstore dataset. The goal of this project is to identify key performance drivers, regional strengths, and areas of high-loss to inform strategic business decisions.

**Key Metrics Tracked:**
* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Sales/Profit by Region, Category, and Ship Mode

---

## Data Source

* **Source File:** `Sample - Superstore.csv`
* **Size:** 9,994 records
* **Scope:** Four years of sales data across multiple regions and product categories.

---

## Power BI Dashboard Highlights

The dashboard is designed for quick insights and provides answers to critical business questions:

1.  **Regional Performance:** Identifies the **West** and **East** regions as the primary profit drivers, highlighting the low margin in the **Central** region.
2.  **Category Efficiency:** Pinpoints **Furniture** as the least profitable category despite significant sales volume.
3.  **Loss Analysis:** Isolates loss-making Sub-Categories like **Binders** and **Tables** for immediate review.

### Tool Used
* **Visualization & Analysis:** Power BI Desktop

---

## Key Insights from the Analysis

* **Profit Concentration:** ~70% of total profit is generated by the West and East regions combined.
* **Margin Erosion:** Five sub-categories (Binders, Tables, Machines, Bookcases, Chairs) are responsible for over $123,000 in net losses.
* **Customer Focus:** The **Consumer** segment is the most valuable, generating the highest percentage of overall profit.
